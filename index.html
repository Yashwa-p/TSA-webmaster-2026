About Us

Newark Nurturing is dedicated to amplifying the voices of local nonprofits through the power of art. We believe creativity drives change — our workshops, collaborations, and community events bring people together to make a difference. A portion of our proceeds directly supports organizations that strengthen our community.

How You Can Help

There are several ways you can make a difference in our community:

  • Volunteer Your Time: Join workshops or help at events to support local nonprofits.
  • Spread Awareness: Share our initiatives and events on social media to amplify impact.
  • Donate Materials: Art supplies, resources, or funds go a long way in supporting our programs.
  • Collaborate With Us: Offer your skills or services to strengthen community projects.
